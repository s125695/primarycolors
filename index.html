Primary Colors

Red, Yellow, and Blue are primary colors. They cannot be made by mixing other colors.

Fun Facts!

  • Red + Yellow = Orange
  • Red + Blue = Purple
  • Yellow + Blue = Green

Secondary colors are made by mixing two primary colors.
